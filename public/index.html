What is Hyperlty?

Hyperlty is a pool for holders. The money from the Hyperlty will be used to buy Blue Chip NFT, which holders will be able to receive in exchange for $Hypers on the raffle platform.

Where will Hyperlty's liquidity come from?

We will monetize our main platforms: launchpad, staking, raffles. Projects that will be hosted on these platforms will pay a commission. Also, all royalties from secondary NFT sales will go to Hyperlty.
